Learn Enhancer

Auto-Login

Experimental! Automatically log in to Learn by clicking on the correct buttons. This relies on your password being saved by Chrome. Depending on your OS, you may need to click into the password field as soon as the page loads so that the password is correctly populated.

Hide Courses

Add the titles of courses to hide them from the sidebar. Useful in second semester when there are courses you no longer care about. Put one course code per line, you may or may not need the semester suffix after the code. So try both SENG365 and SENG365S1.
